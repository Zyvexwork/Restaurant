Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour
Born in Passion

Aurum was born from a singular obsession — the belief that every meal can be a transformative experience. Founded by Chef Dominique Aurèle in the heart of Dubai, we have spent over fifteen years redefining what it means to dine in luxury.

Our philosophy draws from classical French technique, the bold spices of the Middle East, and the pristine produce of the Mediterranean. Each plate is a conversation between tradition and innovation.

Chef Dominique Aurèle

Executive Chef & Founder

The Brigade

Meet the Visionaries

The minds and hands behind every extraordinary dish at Aurum.

Head Chef

Dominique Aurèle

Executive Chef & Founder

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu and Le Bernardin, Chef Aurèle brings 22 years of Michelin-starred mastery. His cuisine is a dialogue between classical French tradition and the vibrant spice routes of the East.

Speciality Modern French · Molecular
Pastry Chef

Sofia Reyes

Head Pastry Chef

A graduate of École Ferrandi Paris, Sofia turns sugar and chocolate into edible sculptures. Her desserts are theatrical, precise and utterly unforgettable.

Speciality Patisserie · Chocolate Art
Sous Chef

Kenji Mori

Sous Chef · Japanese Cuisine

Trained in Tokyo's finest kaiseki restaurants, Kenji brings the precision of Japanese knife skills and the philosophy of wabi-sabi to Aurum's tasting menu.

Speciality Kaiseki · Knife Art

Aurum maintains a smart-elegant dress code. We kindly request gentlemen wear a collared shirt and closed shoes. Formal evening wear is welcomed and encouraged. Sportswear, shorts, and casual sandals are not permitted.

Yes. Aurum offers three private dining rooms for exclusive events — from intimate dinners of six to corporate celebrations of forty. We provide fully bespoke menus, wine pairings, floral arrangements, and dedicated service teams. Contact our events team for availability.

We kindly ask that cancellations be made at least 48 hours before your reservation. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a per-person charge. For private dining bookings, a deposit is required at the time of reservation and is non-refundable within 72 hours of the event.
